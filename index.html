Our Privacy Promise

We believe your automation data is your business. Script Commander is built on a foundation of absolute privacy.

  • No Telemetry: We don't track what scripts you run or how often you use the app.
  • Local First: All your scripts are stored locally on your machine. We never upload your personal tools to our servers.
  • Zero Accounts: No signup required. No email tracking. Just download and automate.
  • Open Source: Don't take our word for it—inspect every line of code on GitHub.
