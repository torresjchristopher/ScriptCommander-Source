The Vulnerability Surface

Forced Retention

UIs are engineered for "stickiness." They want you to stay in their ecosystem, clicking through their menus. Shortcut CLI circumvents this by performing the task directly on your hardware and getting out of your way.

Privacy Sovereignty.

Shortcut CLI is architected to be invisible to your data. No accounts. No tracking. No compromise.

Local-Only Memory

All script metadata and file histories exist exclusively in volatile local memory. Shortcut does not maintain a persistent database of your personal files.

Zero Telemetry

The application contains no analytics agents, tracking pixels, or pings. Your usage patterns and identity remain completely unknown to us.

No Cloud persistence

Shortcut CLI has no home server. We do not offer cloud syncing, ensuring your automated workflows never leave your physical hardware.

Radical Transparency

Absolute privacy requires absolute transparency. The entire codebase is open for inspection on GitHub at all times.
